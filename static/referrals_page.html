How Referrals Work

Our streamlined process makes referring patients simple, secure, and rewarding. Follow these four easy steps to connect your patients with the right specialists.

1

Create Referral

Enter patient information and referral details through our secure platform. Our HIPAA-compliant system ensures all data is protected.

2

Find Specialists

Search our network of qualified specialists by location, specialty, and availability. View profiles and ratings to make informed decisions.

3

Send Referral

Send the referral instantly through our platform. Specialists receive immediate notifications and can respond quickly to schedule appointments.

4

Earn Rewards

Receive points for successful referrals and track patient outcomes. Redeem rewards for cash, credits, or premium features.
