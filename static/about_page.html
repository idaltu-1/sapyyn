About Sapyyn

We're revolutionizing dental referrals with secure technology, seamless workflows, and an integrated rewards system that recognizes excellence in patient care.

Our Mission

Transforming the way dental professionals connect, collaborate, and care for patients nationwide.

Connecting Care, Rewarding Excellence

At Sapyyn, we believe that exceptional patient care should be recognized and rewarded. Our mission is to create a seamless, secure platform that not only streamlines dental referrals but also acknowledges the valuable contributions of dental professionals through our integrated rewards system. We're building a community where quality care and professional excellence are celebrated and compensated.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do and every decision we make.

Security First

We prioritize patient privacy and data security with HIPAA-compliant systems, end-to-end encryption, and rigorous security protocols.

Collaboration

We foster meaningful connections between dental professionals, creating a network that benefits everyone involved in patient care.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our platform with cutting-edge technology to meet the changing needs of modern dental practices.

Excellence

We reward and recognize excellence in patient care through our comprehensive rewards system and quality metrics.

Patient-Centered

Every feature we build and every decision we make is focused on improving patient outcomes and experiences.

Trust

We build lasting relationships based on transparency, reliability, and consistent delivery of exceptional service.

Our Journey

From concept to revolutionizing dental referrals across the nation.

January 2023
Company Founded
Dr. Sarah Johnson and Michael Chen founded Sapyyn with a vision to streamline dental referrals and improve patient care outcomes.
June 2023
Platform Development
Completed initial platform development with HIPAA compliance, secure messaging, and basic referral management features.
October 2023
Beta Launch
Launched beta version with 50 dental practices, receiving valuable feedback and refining core functionalities.
March 2024
Rewards System Launch
Introduced innovative rewards program, making Sapyyn the first platform to gamify dental referrals with meaningful incentives.
September 2024
National Expansion
Reached 500+ dental professionals across 15 states, establishing partnerships with major dental groups and specialty practices.
December 2024
AI Integration
Launched AI-powered referral matching and predictive analytics to optimize patient-specialist connections and outcomes.
