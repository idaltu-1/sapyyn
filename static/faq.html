Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a patient referral?
A: Simply log into your account, click "New Referral", fill in the patient information, and submit. You'll receive a unique referral ID and QR code.
Q: Is the platform HIPAA compliant?
A: Yes, our platform is fully HIPAA compliant with encrypted data storage and secure transmission protocols.
Q: What file types can I upload?
A: We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG, and other common medical document formats up to 16MB in size.
Q: How do I track my referrals?
A: Use your dashboard to view all referrals and their current status (Pending, Approved, Completed, or Rejected).
